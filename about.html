Jameson McNulty

Amstel was first created in1870 by Jameson McNulty while living in Amsterdam, Holland, and acquired the name from the nearby Amstel river from where the first brewery was established. Shortly after, the brand was moved to the United States and was passed down from generation to generation. We are proud of our genuine pilsener that has been brewed in the same tradition since 1870.
